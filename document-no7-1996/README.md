## About this document

Document No. 7 is the Politburo Standing Committee's directive, chaired by Jiang
Zemin, that set PRC policy toward the Uyghur region for the years that followed:
it directs the strengthening of party control down to village level, the
restriction of religious life, controls on publications and cultural activity,
the reinforcement of the Bingtuan and military presence, and diplomatic pressure
on neighboring states against Uyghur organizations abroad. It is among the most
frequently cited primary sources on PRC policy in the region.

An English translation, made from the Chinese version, was published by Human
Rights Watch in March 1998: *China: State Control of Religion, Update Number 1*,
Appendix I, pp. 10–14 —
<https://www.hrw.org/reports/pdfs/c/china/china983.pdf>.
An English translation made directly from this Uyghur version is in preparation.

The Uyghur version transcribed here has, to our knowledge, not previously been
available as searchable text.

## Transcription policy

The transcription reproduces the printed Uyghur text exactly, including the
orthographic conventions of its period and the typographical errors of the
original printing. No modernization, normalization, or correction has been
applied. The transcription was verified against the scan sentence by sentence.

Known typographical errors of the original printing, preserved as printed:

- ئېچى-سىرتىدىكى (for ئىچى-سىرتىدىكى)
- كىرگۈزۈۋلغان (for كىرگۈزۈۋالغان)
- بويرۇق (for بۇيرۇق)
- پۇۋقۇلئاددە (for پەۋقۇلئاددە)
- ھۈججەت (for ھۆججەت)

The scan in this folder is the authority; any discrepancy between transcription
and scan not listed above is a transcription error, and corrections to match the
scan are welcome.
